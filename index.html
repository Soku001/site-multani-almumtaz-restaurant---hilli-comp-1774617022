Our Culinary Philosophy

At Multani AlMumtaz Restaurant, we are passionate about bringing the authentic, vibrant flavors of Pakistani cuisine to Al Ain. Our culinary journey began with a vision to create a space where every dish tells a story of heritage, perfected through generations.

The Chef's Legacy

Our Head Chef, with years of expertise garnered from the heart of Multan, meticulously crafts each dish. Using only the finest, freshest ingredients and traditional techniques, they transform simple spices into a symphony of flavors that excite the palate and warm the soul. It's a dedication to authenticity you can taste in every bite.

Signature Menu Highlights

Indulge in a selection of our most celebrated dishes, crafted with passion and authentic Pakistani spices.

Lahori Charga

A whole chicken marinated in a special blend of Lahori spices, steamed to tenderness, then fried to a crispy golden perfection.

Nihari Gosht

A rich, slow-cooked stew made with succulent beef, infused with aromatic spices, a true Pakistani breakfast delicacy.

Multani Handi Biryani

Fragrant basmati rice cooked with tender meat (chicken/mutton) and a secret blend of spices in a traditional clay pot.

Seekh Kebab Platter

Succulent minced lamb and beef skewers, char-grilled to perfection, bursting with smoky, aromatic flavors.

Haleem Special

A hearty, nutritious stew made from wheat, barley, meat (beef/mutton), and lentils, slow-cooked to a thick, porridge-like consistency.

Peshawari Karahi

A classic chicken or mutton dish, cooked in a wok with fresh tomatoes, green chilies, ginger, and traditional spices, a true comfort food.
